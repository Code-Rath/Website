About CodeRath

CodeRath is a small studio focused on clean, practical tools with predictable, minimal experiences.

CodeRath builds focused digital tools with a strong emphasis on clarity, speed and stability. From finance utilities to small daily helpers, each product is designed to feel light and easy to trust.

The current focus is Android utility apps, with room for desktop tools and AI‑assisted utilities in the future. The goal is to remove friction from everyday tasks rather than add more noise.
