Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how CodeRath (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information in its apps and on this website. It currently applies to apps including EMI & SIP Calculator By RR and Toolzy – Calculator & Tools (once released), as well as any related tools or forms.

Information we collect

Our apps are designed primarily as utility tools. Most calculations (for example EMI or SIP inputs) are processed directly on your device and are not sent to our own servers.

  • App usage and diagnostics – Some analytics or crash reporting tools may collect anonymous information about app usage or technical issues to help improve stability.
  • Advertising identifiers – If the app shows ads (for example through AdMob or other ad networks), those services may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in their own policies.
  • Form submissions – When you contact us using Microsoft Forms or similar services, the data you submit (such as your email address and message) is stored by that service and shared with us so we can respond.

How we use information

  • To provide core features like calculations, converters and utilities within the apps.
  • To improve app performance, stability and user experience over time.
  • To respond to support requests, feature requests and bug reports you send via forms or email.

Third‑party services

Our apps and this website may rely on third‑party services for analytics, crash reporting or advertising. These services have their own privacy policies and may collect information independently.

  • Advertising networks such as Google AdMob / AdSense.
  • Analytics or crash reporting services (if enabled in the future).
  • Form services such as Microsoft Forms used for contact requests.

Data retention

We keep form submissions and related communication only for as long as necessary to handle your request or maintain reasonable records of support. You can request that we remove your contact details from our records unless we are required to keep them for legal reasons.

Your choices

  • You can uninstall any app at any time from your device.
  • You can decline or reset ad personalization in your device’s system settings or Google account where supported.
  • You may choose not to submit any personal information through forms; however, that may limit our ability to respond to your request.

Children’s privacy

Our apps and this website are not primarily targeted at children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our forms, please contact us so we can review and handle the situation appropriately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as apps or services change. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
